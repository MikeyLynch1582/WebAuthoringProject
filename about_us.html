Breezy is a sports/activewear shop which is located in the centre of the town in Kilmallock, Co.Limerick. Here at Breezy, our main aim is to contact with all groups of demographics and keep them motivated to stay fit and active with the use of our goods. We feel like a healthy body leads to a healthy mind which leads then to a healthy mentality on life itself. We want to provide the best of goods and materials to help every person on each of their individual journeys.

Our business was established by Mikey Lynch in 2012. Mikey Lynch is a business man from Athlacca, Co.Limerick. Mikey created all aspects of the business from the creation of the logo, slogan and name to the colour scheme, digital marketing and socials. The business now employees up to 20 people in the local area and has a work at home option which a team of 10 people are doing.
